Hi, I am Jinsoo, an Inclusion Support Coach, passionate about helping people gain their confidence when and where they do not feel belonged due to their perceived and actual differences.

I am currently offering the following coaching services via phone or Zoom. Please inquire at jinsoochacoaching@gmail.com for the free initial interest session to discuss your goals and challenges so that we can create a plan together.

Inclusion Support Coaching

You might be an expat who just moved to another country. You might consider yourself a minority in your current living or work situations. Whoever you might be, you do not feel included and belonged and that is impacting your happiness and even career. As an Inclusion Support Coach, I am here to help you.

  • Do you feel unprepared and unconfident in your personal and professional life?
  • Do you feel reluctant to speak up?
  • Do you feel that your work is not getting fully acknowledged, recognized, and compensated?
  • Do you feel stuck in the same role?
  • Do you want to improve your performance and communication?
  • Do you want to advance in your career?

Here's how I can help you.

  • I can help you gain clarity in where you are in your career and life.
  • I can help you reduce the gap and find the context by understanding where the challenges are really coming from.
  • I can help you obtain a new insight into where you want to be in your career and life.
  • I can help you form specific action plans regarding how you would overcome the challenges and make progress.
  • I can help you feel confident by providing full support in preparing for circumstances where you feel unprepared and uncomfortable.

If you are ready to move forward and make progress in your life and career by overcoming the challenges that you are facing at the moment, I would be happy to provide supports in your journey! Please send me an email at jinsoochacoaching@gmail.com for your initial interest session.

Pro Bono Coaching

I believe that everyone needs access to coaching. I offer pro bono coaching for a small one time administration fee of $15 as a way of embracing those who need support, but cannot afford conventional coaching services. Clients who apply and are accepted into the program will receive a weekly 1 hour coaching session for 6 weeks in regards to any topics that a client and I find suitable.
