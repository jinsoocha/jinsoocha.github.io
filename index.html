I am currently offering the following coaching services via phone or Zoom. Please inquire at jinsoochacoaching@gmail.com for the free initial interest session to discuss your goals and challenges so that we can create a plan together.

Inclusion Support Coaching

You might be an expat who just has transitioned into a new role in another country. You might be considered a minority in your current living or work situations. Whoever you might be, you do not feel included and belonged. And as an Inclusion Support Coach, I am here to help you.

  • Do you feel unprepared and unconfident in your personal and professional life?
  • Do you feel that your work is not getting fully acknowledged, recognized, and compensated?
  • Do you feel stuck in the same role?
  • Do you want to improve your performance and communication?
  • Do you want to advance in your career?

Pro Bono Coaching

I believe that everyone needs access to coaching. I offer pro bono coaching for a small one time administration fee of $40 as a way of embracing those who need support, but cannot afford conventional coaching services. Clients who apply and are accepted into the program will receive a weekly 1 hour coaching session for 6 weeks in regards to any topics that a client and I find suitable.
