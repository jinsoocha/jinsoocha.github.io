I am currently offering the following coaching services via phone or Zoom. Please inquire at jinsoochacoaching@gmail.com for the free initial strategy session to discuss your goals and challenges so that we can create a plan together.

Expat Transition Coaching

  • Do you feel unprepared and unconfident in your personal and professional life as an expat?
  • Do you feel that your work is not getting fully acknowledged, recognized, and compensated?
  • Do you want to improve your performance and communication at work as an expat?

Financial Mindfulness Coaching

  • Do you feel unsure about where your money is going?
  • Do you feel that your money is controlling you rather than you have control over your financial life?
  • Do you want to improve your relationship with money?

Pro Bono Coaching

I believe that everyone needs access to coaching. I offer pro bono coaching for a small one time administration fee of $40 as a way of embracing those who need support, but cannot afford conventional coaching services. Clients who apply and are accepted into the program will receive a weekly 1 hour coaching session for 6 weeks in regards to any topics that a client wants to discuss.
